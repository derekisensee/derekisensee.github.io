Support

Crossgrid Words

Help for the iPhone and iPad word puzzle game Crossgrid Words.

Need Help?

If something is broken, confusing, or missing, please open an issue on GitHub and include your device, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Support and bug reports: github.com/derekisensee/derekisensee.github.io/issues

Common Questions

  • Daily boards are shared by date, so reinstalling the app does not change the daily puzzle for that day.
  • Progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes local progress and history.
  • The game is designed to work offline once installed.

Privacy Policy

Crossgrid Words does not collect personal information, does not use tracking, and does not include third-party analytics in the iPhone or iPad app.

Game progress, settings, daily history, campaign progress, and library completion are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, that local data may be removed by iOS.

The app does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email address, and does not access your photos, contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

If you choose to contact support or open a GitHub issue, the information you provide there may be visible on GitHub depending on how you submit it. Please avoid including private personal information in public support requests.

About The App

Crossgrid Words is a word puzzle game about building valid words through the letters already on the board. The app includes campaign levels, a large simple level library, shared daily boards, endless play, and seed-based runs.
