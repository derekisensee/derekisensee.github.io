Crossgrid Words

Help for the iPhone and iPad word puzzle game Crossgrid Words.

Need Help?

If something is broken, confusing, or missing, please open an issue on GitHub and include your device, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Support and bug reports: github.com/derekisensee/derekisensee.github.io/issues

Common Questions

  • Daily boards are shared by date, so reinstalling the app does not change the daily puzzle for that day.
  • Progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes local progress and history.
  • The game is designed to work offline once installed.

Privacy

Crossgrid Words has a separate privacy policy covering local progress, tracking data, advertising, analytics, and support requests.

Read the Crossgrid Words Privacy Policy

About The App

Crossgrid Words is a word puzzle game about building valid words through the letters already on the board. The app includes campaign levels, a large simple level library, shared daily boards, endless play, and seed-based runs.
