Crossgrid Words

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026.

This privacy policy explains how Crossgrid Words handles information in the iPhone and iPad app.

Information We Collect

Crossgrid Words does not collect personal information from you. The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details.

The app does not access your photos, contacts, camera, microphone, location, Bluetooth, calendars, reminders, health data, or other personal device data.

Local Game Data

Crossgrid Words stores game progress locally on your device. This may include campaign progress, level completion, daily history, library completion, best scores, best times, settings, and seed run history.

Local game data is used only to make the app work, restore your progress, and show your completion history inside the app. If you delete the app, iOS may remove this local data.

Tracking Data

Crossgrid Words does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. The app does not use the Identifier for Advertisers, does not request App Tracking Transparency permission, and does not share data with data brokers or advertising networks.

Crossgrid Words does not include third-party analytics in the iPhone or iPad app. Gameplay events, level results, daily plays, and completion history stay local to your device.

Advertising

Crossgrid Words does not currently show advertisements. The app does not use ad SDKs, ad networks, ad attribution tools, or personalized advertising systems.

Because the app does not serve ads, it does not collect or share information for advertising targeting, ad measurement, or retargeting.

Support Requests

If you choose to contact support or open a GitHub issue, the information you provide there may be visible on GitHub depending on how you submit it. Please avoid including private personal information in public support requests.

Changes To This Policy

If Crossgrid Words changes how it handles data, tracking, analytics, or advertising, this policy will be updated to reflect those changes.
